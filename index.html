Hello + welcome!

My name is Cally.

I am a full-time mama and a part-time photographer! I am in my early thirties, married to my best friend, and our little family resides in Chilliwack, BC. I have been photographing families & fellow photographers in the Fraser Valley area for almost ten years now, and I truly cherish each soul I meet and each story I get the opportunity of telling.

I offer both outdoor & in-home lifestyle portrait sessions that meet you where you’re at; whether it’s family photos, branding portraits, celebrating a milestone, or welcoming the newest member of your family. I invite you to learn more about the packages I offer below to see which will be the best fit for you. I am looking forward to connecting with you and working together to capture your dream session!

Monthly Minis

Quick & memorable sessions to celebrate those you love.

I am pleased to provide year-round minis for my fellow Lower-Mainland folks!

These mini sessions are the perfect opportunity to capture the in-betweens of daily life, and also to celebrate milestone moments. You have your choice of 10 different mini session dates spread throughout the year, at set locations & times. These lifestyle sessions are open to singles, couples, & small families of five or less. Each mini session is tailored to you, wherever you are at in life!

  • An outdoor 25-minute session
  • A pre-session "tips" e-booklet
  • Styling recommendations
  • 40 hi-res images

Prior to your session I will be in touch to help with all the details such as outfit-planning, where to meet, what to expect, tips & tricks etc. Reservations are taken directly online, but don’t hesitate to reach out if you have any questions. It is my priority to provide you with a fun & smooth experience resulting in a beautiful gallery of warm & memorable images that will want you coming back for more!
