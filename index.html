Hello + welcome!

My name is Cally.

I am a full-time mama and a part-time photographer! I am in my early thirties, married to my best friend, and our little family resides in Chilliwack, BC. I have been photographing families & fellow photographers in the Fraser Valley area for ten years now, and I truly cherish each soul I meet and each story I get the opportunity of telling.

I offer both outdoor & in-home lifestyle portrait sessions that meet you where you’re at; whether it’s family photos, branding portraits, celebrating a milestone, or welcoming the newest member of your family. I invite you to learn more about the packages I offer below to see which will be the best fit for you. I am looking forward to connecting with you and working together to capture your dream session!

Memories on the Vedder

A cozy & sweet photo-experience on the Vedder River, Chilliwack BC.

This session is geared towards families who need a little extra support throughout this season, whatever that means for you.

Our little family moved to Chilliwack recently and we have absolutely fallen in love with the Vedder River. Because we now live only a few short minutes from this location, I have the opportunity to offer this package at an amazing low rate! The river is beautiful all times of the year; a breathtaking view even on the most dreary of days. It is truly the perfect place to document memories with loved ones.

The investment & what’s included…

  • Location: Vedder River, Chilliwack
  • A slow-paced 30 min session, open to small families of 5 or less
  • You set the date & pace
  • I guide the style, posing, & process
  • 50+ hi-res images delivered within 1-2 weeks
